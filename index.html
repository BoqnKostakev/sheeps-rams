sheeps vs rams

Sheeps and Rams (also known as Bulls and Cows) is an old code-breaking game. A random four-digit number (secret number) with different digits is generated automatically. Your goal is to guess that number. Enter four different digits in the field below. The leftmost digit must not be 0 (zero).

Sheep means that a digit from your number is contained in secret number, but not on the same position. If two such digits exists, the sheep are 2.

Ram means that a digit from your number is contained in secret number and it is on the same position. If two such digits exists, the rams are 2.
